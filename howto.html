How to play the game

  • When you press start, the game is initializedm with 60 seconds and 0 points
  • The game time will be decremanting every second. Once your time or points are below 0, then the game is over.
  • In order to avoid time loss, you have to aim to eat as many burgers as possible using the SpongeBob character.
  • You use the direction buttons or the keyboard arrow keys on a PC to control your SpongeBob character
  • Every burger eaten results in a time increment of 10 seconds and a point incriment of 10 points as well
  • If you hit a wall you get a sscore decrement of 0
  • You can play the game for as long as you wish. If you decide to quit, you can just press the stop button.
  • For your name to appear in the leaderboard you have to score at least 100 points.
  • If you did not provide your name at the begining of the game, you will appear as Stranger101 in the leader board

Enjoy the game!
